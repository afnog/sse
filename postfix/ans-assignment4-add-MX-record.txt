#### Add an MX record #####

	#cd /etc/namedb/master

	#ee XXX.ws.renu.ac.ug


At the bottom of the file add the following lines


	mail IN A 196.43.185.2XX
	@ IN MX 10 mail.XXX.ws.renu.ac.ug.
	@ IN MX 20 mail.YYY.ws.renu.ac.ug.


Replace XXX with your zone name.

Incremenent the Serial number then save and exit the file

Reload BIND as follows

	#rndc reload

	OR

	#service named reload
